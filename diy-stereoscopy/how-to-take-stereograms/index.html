How To Take Stereograms

Ever wanted to take you own stereographic pairs and create 3D images? With the power of your smartphone, taking stereographic photos is easier than one might think.

With i3DSteroid or CrossCam, taking stereographic photos is easy.

How to take photos with i3Dsteroid.

i3DSteroid is an iPhone-only, $2.99 app that allows its users to easily take 3D photos. The app interface is generally pretty intuitive.

If you want to download the app, please click here. A link to the user guide can be found here.

How to take photos with CrossCam

CrossCam is a free, Android and iPhone-supporting app that has a sleeker design and is therefore a little more difficult to understand. Not only does it let users take stereographic photos within the app itself, it also allows its users to upload their own stereopairs if they already have them.
